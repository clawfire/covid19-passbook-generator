EU Digital COVID Certificate Passbook
What's that project?

This web application is made by EU citizens in order to let you scan your EU Digital COVID certificate (vaccine, test or healing) and generate a passbook from it.

This application is working 100% on your device and store nothing remotly.
However, Apple requires the passbook to be signed and that cannot happen in your device without compromising the signing key.

All the source code can be read and audited right here

How does it work?

Scan
Point your device to your EU Digital COVID certificate
Signature
We are decoding the code, verifying the information and generating a passbook. We're sending the passbook to our server for signature. We don't store anything.
Download
Your final passbook is ready. Add it to your wallet.
Aim at the QR Code of your certificate to capture.
